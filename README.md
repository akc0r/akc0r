## 🖋️ About Me

> **Software Engineer · SRE · AI Engineer — building systems that are fast, reliable, and intelligent.**

I design and operate production-grade systems end to end — from reactive front-ends (Next.js / Tailwind) to distributed backends and AI pipelines. As an SRE, I make reliability a first-class concern: zero-downtime deployments, deep observability stacks (Prometheus / Grafana), and incident-proof architectures. As an AI Engineer, I integrate machine learning into real products that deliver measurable value.

My edge is working across the full stack — systems-level precision (C/C++), scalable services, and production ML — without losing sight of the user.
